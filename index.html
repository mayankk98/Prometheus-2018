About

Prometheus is the annual Technical Fest of IECSE. This is the seventh time the fest will be organized by the club. The technical extravaganza will begin on the 23rd of March and will continue till the 25th. We have planned seven events for Prometheus VII which includes both technical and non technical competitions. In the previous years we have seen participation in mass numbers in all events and we expect an even greater turn up this time.

It's 'The Seventh Expedition' this time. So as we say,
Let the games begin!

Events

Aegon's Conquest

The lands of Westeros have long been in chaos, consisting of quarrelsome kingdoms ruled by power hungry patriarchs with massive armies. Help Aegon Targaryen, the male scion of a once powerful house and his two sisters take the seven kingdoms with fire and blood. From the Stormlands to the Rock, from the Wall to King's Landing. But heed, for conquering is not an elementary task, this event will test your coding prowess over two rounds.

Mystery Room

Ever thought you were ratiocinative enough to outwit and dominate challenges or puzzles? Bring your intuitive skills and best team together while stepping into Mystery Room in an attempt to locate the hints and resolve the crisis! From clues hidden in the room to simple analytical based questions, these tests are sure to assess your out-of-the-box thinking and teamwork skills alike.

Online Coding

Here's your chance to become an equal to Poseidon, the God of the Seven Seas, defeating monsters and obstacles on the way with the wit and logic of your mind. The sail shall be treacherous if you are not familiar with your art of code hence be careful of unexpected trouble. So finesse your skills and get ready to conquer the Seven Seas!

Artem

Let your passion for graphic designing be put to test in this 2-day design event sponsored by The Souled Store. Bring out the saiyan in you and venture in your own journey of creativity with Shenron to collect 'them' all.

HAWKE7E

We often marvel at the technology and knowledge our ancestors used to make great monuments ages ago. However, the lost pages of history have a different story to tell. They speak of an ancient being, one who guided the hand of men and helped them excel through the course of history leading to the modern world we live in today. Take a journey through the ages with one mission. To find... The Hawk

GamBit

Think you can code? Come bet on it against every other coder in the room at the most fast-paced and thrilling coding event you've ever attended. Keep your fingers crossed and we'll keep you on your toes till the very end.

Bamboozled

An expedition filled with commotion and havoc, Bamboozled brings to you a platform to show off your problem solving and analytical skills. Put your game face on, get a friend along and join us on a frenzied adventure to Paradise Pond.
